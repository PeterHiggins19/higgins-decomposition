  THE BALUN PROOF — Cross-Domain Summary
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  A balun transformer converts between balanced and unbalanced signals.
  In RF engineering:
    - Matched: Γ → 0, VSWR → 1, all power transferred
    - Mismatched: Γ → 1, VSWR → ∞, power reflected back

  In Hˢ, the Trace operator IS the balun:
    - Balanced input:  V(t) ∈ Sym_D  (D×D covariance, differential mode)
    - Unbalanced out:  Tr(V(t)) ∈ ℝ  (scalar trajectory, common mode)
    - Matched: diagonal dominates → Γ small → clean scalar extraction
    - Mismatched: off-diagonal dominates → Γ large → information lost
